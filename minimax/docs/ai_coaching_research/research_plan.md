## Task Overview
Research specific website requirements and features needed for AI trainers and coaching service providers.

## Research Objectives
1. Analyze successful AI training/coaching websites and their key features
2. Research essential functionalities for service-based businesses (booking systems, payment processing, client portals)
3. Study lead generation and conversion optimization strategies for coaching services

5. Research content marketing strategies for AI education (blogs, resources, case studies)
6. Analyze pricing models and service presentation approaches
7. Study client onboarding and communication workflows
